自2003年成立以来，广源行一直秉承"精诚合作，携手共赢"的企业理念，致力于成为一家集研发、生产、销售为一体的专业金属加工制造商。公司位于江苏省昆山市，占地4,500平方米，拥有60余项发明及实用新型专利。


核心产品：汽车配件（汽车饰条、电池镍片、铰链等）、半导体晶圆框架、灯具五金、卫浴五金、家具五金、铰链、控制器盒拉深件、电子类产品五金配件、新能源电池配件等。

Since our founding in 2003, GYH has upheld the philosophy of "Sincere Cooperation, Win-Win Together," committed to becoming a fully integrated metal processing manufacturer combining R&D, production, and sales. Located in Kunshan, Jiangsu Province, our 4,500 m² facility houses 60+ invention and utility model patents.


Core Products: Automotive Parts, Semiconductor Wafer Frames, Lighting Hardware, Bathroom & Sanitary Hardware, Furniture Fittings, Controller Box Parts, Electronic Hardware, and New Energy Battery Accessories.
